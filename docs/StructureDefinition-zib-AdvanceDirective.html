Resource Profile: HCIM AdvanceDirective

Official URL: http://nictiz.nl/fhir/StructureDefinition/zib-AdvanceDirective Version: 1.3.15
Active as of 2024-04-09 Computable Name: Zib AdvanceDirective

Copyright/Legal: CC0

AdvanceDirective (formerly know as LivingWill) as defined by the Dutch Health and Care Information models (Dutch: Zorginformatiebouwsteen or ZIB) version 3.1. A living will is a verbal or written description of the patient’s wishes with regard to future medical action or end of their life. A living will is mainly used for situations in which the patient is no longer able to speak about these decisions with their healthcare provider.

A good overview of the patient’s wishes is important. A healthcare provider can use the living will to discuss the existing wishes with the persons involved and to incorporate these wishes in their own policy. A concise, orderly list is of particular importance in acute care situations.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: HCIM TreatmentDirective
