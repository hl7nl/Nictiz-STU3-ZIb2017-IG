Resource Profile: HCIM VaccinationRecommendation

Official URL: http://nictiz.nl/fhir/StructureDefinition/zib-VaccinationRecommendation Version: 1.3.15
Active as of 2024-04-09 Computable Name: Zib VaccinationRecommendation

Copyright/Legal: CC0

This profile is one of two profile based on Vaccination as defined by the Dutch Health and Care Information models (Dutch: Zorginformatiebouwsteen or ZIB) version 3.1. This profile is also used for the Dutch Health and Care Information model PlannedCareActivityForTransfer version 3.1

The ZIB vaccinatie defines the concept DesiredDateForRevaccination. FHIR has a dedicated resources for this. To cover DesiredDateForRevaccination and to exand on the ZIB vaccination, the ImmunizationRecommendation Resource is used and profiled.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: ImmunizationRecommendation/zib-vaccinationrecommendation-01
