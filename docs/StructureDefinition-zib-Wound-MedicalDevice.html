Resource Profile: HCIM Wound MedicalDevice

Official URL: http://nictiz.nl/fhir/StructureDefinition/zib-Wound-MedicalDevice Version: 1.3.15
Draft as of 2023-10-17 Computable Name: Zib Wound MedicalDevice

A Device as defined by the Dutch HCIM Zorginformatiebouwsteen Wond v3.1(2017EN). A drain is a medical aid placed to drain blood and exudate from the wound area.

Profiles purpose is to define the usage of a wounddrain.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: DeviceUseStatement/zib-wound-medicaldevice-01
