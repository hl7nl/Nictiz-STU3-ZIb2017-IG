Resource Profile: HCIM BodyTemperature

Official URL: http://nictiz.nl/fhir/StructureDefinition/zib-BodyTemperature Version: 1.3.15
Active as of 2024-04-09 Computable Name: Zib BodyTemperature

Copyright/Legal: CC0

This profile is derived from the HL7 Vital Signs profile for Body temperature in order to add mappings from the Dutch Health and Care Information models (Dutch: Zorginformatiebouwsteen or ZIB) BodyTemperature v3.1, release 2017, and to provide extensions for missing concepts.

Measuring the body temperature is of medical importance. A number of diseases are accompanied by characteristic changes in body temperature. The symptoms of some diseases can be tracked by measuring the body temperature. The healthcare provider can use this temperature development to determine the effect of the treatment.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Observation/zib-bodytemperature-01
